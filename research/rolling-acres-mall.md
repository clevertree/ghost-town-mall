# Ghost Town Mall — Research: Rolling Acres Mall (Akron, OH)

Primary real-world inspiration. Rolling Acres Mall in Akron, Ohio.

## Timeline

- Opened **1975** — 1.3 million sq. ft., 2-story, state-of-the-art
- Declined through the 1980s–90s
- Mid-90s renovation attempt failed to attract customers back
- Died in **2005**
- Demolition began afterward

## Key Events

- **Richard Cooey** — connected to crimes near the mall
  - Two women found slain in Norton, Sept. 2, 1986
- **Body found hanging from tree** near Rolling Acres (Jan. 7, 2016) — ruled a suicide
- **Richard Beasley**, the "Craigslist Killer," used the area around the mall

> "Rich pulls back a branch and lets it slap back at Brogan and Tim. Then Tim holds it back for Brogan so he can walk by. This act of kindness disturbs Brogan, though he doesn't say anything."
